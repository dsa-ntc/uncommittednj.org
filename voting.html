Voting F.A.Q

===

Can I vote if i have never voted before?

If you are a registered deomocrat but have never voted before you can vote in the primary. To check your registration status visit this link Check Voter Registeration

What happens if i am unafilliated?

If you are a unaffiliated you can still vote in the democratic primary. If you vote in the democratic primary you will be switched to the democratic party for your registration. If you wish to change this after you vote you may do so here. Change Political Party

Where can i find my polling place?

New Jersey has created a portal to find your place to vote. Find Polling PLace
