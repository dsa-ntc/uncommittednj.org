How to Vote Uncommitted

If you're a registered Democrat in New Jersey, all you need to do is Vote "Uncommitted" for the presidential nominee in the NJ Primary Election.

Not registered to vote, or registered as Unaffiliated? You can register online to change your party affiliation before the election! If you're already registered as Unaffiliated, you can request a Democratic primary ballot from a poll worker!

Looking for Uncommitted on your ballot? Take a look at our sample ballot images to find Uncommitted on your mail-in ballot, or to prepare to vote in person on June 4th.
